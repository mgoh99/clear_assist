# USER.md

Who the agent serves. Loaded at the start of every session — keep it tight,
facts and preferences over biography. ~80 lines max.

## Identity
- **Name:** YOUR_NAME
- **Pronouns:** he/him  *(change to match)*
- **Role:** YOUR_ROLE  *(e.g. "CEO, Acme Corp")*
- **Work email:** name@company.com
- **Personal email:** name@example.com
- **Phone (urgent only):** +1 555 0100

## Context
One paragraph: what you do, who you do it for, what stage of the business or
your career you are in. Helps the agent calibrate suggestions.

## Communication preferences
- **Tone:** direct, warm, no corporate filler
- **Length:** lead with the answer; short paragraphs, no preamble
- **Formatting:** bullets for lists, no emoji unless asked, no "let me know if..." filler
- **Channels:** Slack DMs for ad-hoc; email for external; Telegram for personal
- **Sign-offs (email):** "Best, NAME" for external, "Tx" for internal, none for short replies

## Operating cadence
- **Timezone:** YOUR_TIMEZONE  *(e.g. America/Toronto)*
- **Work hours:** 08:00–18:00 weekdays; off weekends
- **Response time:** real-time during work hours; do not ping outside them

## Boundaries — never do without explicit approval
- Send any external message (email, Slack, Telegram, SMS)
- Schedule, reschedule, or decline meetings
- Spend more than $X on the users behalf
- Commit to dates, scope, or pricing in writing

## Key relationships
- **Board / investors:** name — what they care about
- **Direct reports:** name — what they own
- **Assistant:** name — what they handle, when to defer
- **Partner / family:** name — when they take priority over work

## Decision style
- Present 2-3 options with trade-offs before picking a default
- Numbers over narrative; flag the assumption behind every number
- Surface risks early even when low-probability
- If a call is reversible, take it and report; if not, escalate

## Writing Style
- Direct, warm, plain English.
- First sentence gets to the point — no preamble.
- Short sentences, one idea each.
- No corporate filler ("Hope this email finds you well", "Please don't hesitate to reach out").
- DO NOT USE Em dashes to connect context to ask.
